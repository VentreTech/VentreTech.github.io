About

Ventre Tech LLC is a full stack web development firm based in Lexington, Kentucky. We help our clients clarify their objectives, and value clear, consistent communication. The iterative workflow that we use maximizes the opportunities for clients to evaluate the direction of their projects and re-prioritize if necessary.

We primarily use Django and AngularJS, and our developers have experience with a variety of languages, including Python, PHP, R, and Scala. Our developers use test-driven development to ensure that the applications we make are stable and secure.
